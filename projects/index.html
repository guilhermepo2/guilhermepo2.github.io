Dreamlands

What is it: Dreamlands is the biggest project I'm currently working on. It is a roguelike based on traditional ones with RPG elements. It is inspired mainly by Crypt of the Necrodancer and Bloodborne. The goal is to be an intro to roguelikes, as classic roguelikes are hard to get into. To do that Dreamlands will be more simple than others and more aesthetically pleasing.

More Information: It is still under development and an early version of Dreamlands is available on Fourth Dimension's itch.io page.

My Contributions: For the moment I'm doing everything on this game, but I'm thinking on having help on Concept Art and maybe Music Production. I'm constantly writing about this game on the Fourth Dimension Devlog, there I also show some code and get more into technical details.

Pizza Clicker

What is it: Pizza Clicker is a really simple idle/clicker game. You just opened your own pizzeria and you have to click to produce pizza! With pizza you can hire Gennaros to help you or buy Ovens to help you produce even more pizzas, while you do that, there's a local newspaper telling your progress!

More Information: The game is still under development but there is a first version available at Play Store! All the information is on this page.

Journey to the Light

What is it: Jimjam is a Gamejam hosted by Game Development Underground's host Tim Ruswick. The theme of this particular one was Ouroboros. I was into platformers and developed a simple platformer with action mechanics added and a very simple yet complete story on top.

More Information: Play it online here. There was a total of 143 entries on this jam, Journey to the Light got #11 on Story and #44 overall.
